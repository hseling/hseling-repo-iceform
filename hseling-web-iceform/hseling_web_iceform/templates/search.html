Search the Corpus

The algorithm for determining significant repetitions is based on the formation of a list of grams and its further filtering. Then the constructs are grouped together based on semantic and contextual similarity.

This search provides the ability to search by semantic groups. You can filter constructions by the total frequency of the constructions included in the group and the number of texts in which they occur. Each construction presented in the output is one of the examples that is included in the group. Clicking on one of the output phases will allow you to see all the constructs in the group and the contexts in which they occur.

Output is filtered by the first letter of the verb.
